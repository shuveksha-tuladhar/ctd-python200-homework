Groundwork Coffee Co. — Summer Community Event

Join us this Saturday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM for our Summer Community Coffee Day.

We will be offering:
- Free coffee tastings of our seasonal cold brews
- Live acoustic music from local artists
- A latte art workshop (free, first come first served)

This event is open to everyone and is designed to celebrate our local community and introduce new seasonal drinks.

Location: All Groundwork Coffee Co. locations
